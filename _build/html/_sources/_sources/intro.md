# Welcome to Fluid Mechanics at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Facultad de Minas

My name is **Carlos D. Hoyos**, and I am one of the professors guiding you through the principles and applications of fluid mechanics. I want to start developing a set of online course materials that will support our in-class lectures, and this content is accessible through Jupyter Books hosted on GitHub.


## The Importance of Fluid Mechanics

Fluid mechanics is a fundamental discipline that underpins many engineering applications, from energy generation and environmental management to aerospace and biomedical engineering. Understanding fluid behavior helps us design efficient systems, manage natural resources, and solve critical problems in both natural and engineered environments. Mastering fluid mechanics is essential for any engineer looking to tackle challenges related to energy, water, transportation, and more.


## Why Jupyter Books?

Jupyter Books offer a powerful and flexible way to create open, interactive content. They allow us to integrate live code, visualizations, and equations directly into the course notes, making it easier to explore complex concepts interactively. This format aligns well with our goals in fluid mechanics, where mathematical analysis and computational tools are key to solving real-world problems.

## Why GitHub?

GitHub serves as a collaborative platform where version control ensures that the material remains up to date and accessible to everyone. It's a place where you can not only view the notes but also track changes, suggest improvements, and even contribute to the development of new content. By hosting the materials on GitHub, I am embracing a more transparent, community-oriented approach to learning, where your feedback and participation can directly influence the course.

## Why the Notes in English?

Although this course is taught in Spanish, the notes are provided in English to give you exposure to technical terminology that you will encounter in international literature and research. Fluid mechanics is a global discipline, and most academic papers, textbooks, and research in this field are published in English. By engaging with the material in English, you will be better prepared to participate in international conferences, read cutting-edge research, and collaborate with a global scientific community.
